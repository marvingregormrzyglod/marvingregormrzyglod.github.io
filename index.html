Marvin Gregor Mrzyglod has developed blockbuster video games, pioneered practical use of Self-Determination Theory and led design of Sony's oldest exclusive PlayStation franchise. He quit gaming to develop new ways of experiencing the Bible and do missionary work.

He has worked with a range of different languages and tools. He has the most experience with: LUA, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, git, Unreal Engine, Anvil/Scimitar, GameMaker, GML Script and Eschatology.
