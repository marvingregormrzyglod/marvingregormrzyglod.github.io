Game Developer
& Bible Nerd

Marvin Gregor Mrzyglod is a seasoned game developer who spearheaded design for Sony PlayStation’s longest-standing exclusive franchise. A pioneer in applying Self-Determination Theory to gaming, he recently left the industry to pursue his faith, now channeling his talents into Christian work.

He has worked with a range of different languages and tools: LUA, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, git, Unreal Engine, Anvil/Scimitar, GameMaker, and GML Script.

Rational Level Design (RLD)

Concordia University

https://gamecreatorodyssey.com/

2019

  • A joint creation of Ubisoft and Concordia University, Rational Level Design (RLD) leverages thirty years of level design expertise.
  • This method crafts predictable player outcomes through strategic use of visual cues and psychological principles, converting game environments into numerical data for accurate behavior prediction and difficulty tuning.
