Game Developer,
Systems Designer
& Bible Nerd

Marvin Gregor Mrzyglod has developed blockbuster video games, pioneered practical use of Self-Determination Theory and was entrusted with design leadership of Sony's oldest exclusive PlayStation franchise. However, he quit gaming to develop new ways of interacting with the Bible and experience scripture in refreshing ways.

He has worked with a range of different languages and tools. The things he currently has the most experience with are: LUA, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, git, Unreal Engine, Anvil/Scimitar, GameMaker, GML Script and Eschatology.
